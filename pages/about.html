Our Purpose

Our main goal with Book Notes & Vault is primarily to encourage people to read and to discover new literature.

It also serves as a platform to take notes while reading, allowing users to track their sessions and make notetaking more seamless than ever.

"I do believe something magical can happen when you read."

-J.K. Rowling-

Our Vision

We foresee a world where everyone can find a book to their taste. We believe that literature can be the pillar of a greater future.
